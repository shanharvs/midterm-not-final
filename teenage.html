Teenage Years

When was in teenage years, she started to notice time was moving too fast.1 One blink, she’s finally a teenager.2 At those times, she started having crushes and flirts with random boys at her compound.3 She went with her nieces, which is around her age too, to go to them and try to get noticed by those boys.4 She still doesn’t like going to school and notices that she hates Mondays because it’s the first day to attend her classes.5 She’s a late-night crammer but still passes her quizzes and exams.6 Her favorite subject is Araling Panlipunan, especially when the lessons are all about history.7 She’s good at memorizing if she takes it seriously, you see.8 During those school days, she met her new two friends, they talked like typical teenage girls and go out to buy street foods after their class.9 She has had a good social life before and it was thrilling. She also discovers how to read on Wattpad online.10 And she guiltily admits that she reads books that were not suitable for her age but she loves it.11 She still reads her sisters’ books behind their backs and imagines herself being the main love interest in those books.12 Her and her sisters are getting along, or maybe she was a brat when she was a child which is why they were not close before.13 And like some teenagers, she unfortunately experienced bullying.14 She was bullied because she sat with one of the boys in her classroom and the girl thinks she likes him.15 It was one of her most traumatic events that happened in her teenage years. Almost every classmate of hers turned their backs on her.16 And one time, she heard one of her favorite teachers in AP call her ‘bangag’ because she has eyebags under her eyes.17 That was the time she realized that even those you look up to are awful and that respect begets respect.18 Those times ruined her teenage years for her.19 Issues from her family had risen then and there.20 Her Dad also slowly went away and lived in Zamboanga City.21 It was a good thing her family and some of her friends are there to support her in those years and that she graduated with honors in her junior high in JSHS and graduated with high honor in senior high in NCS located in Quiapo, Manila.22 She thinks, her teenage years are is black and white as she experienced terrific happiness and lots of sadness.23
